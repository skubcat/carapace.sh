Roasted Habanero Mango Hotsauce

1x Mango
1x Roasted Habanero
5x Small Bellpepers
1x Medium Onion
2 tsp Sugar
1 tsp Garlic powder

Roast bellpepers 20 mins at 450F.
Vinegar in a pot with ingredients, enough to cover. Boil the mixture for about 5 minutes. 
Blend until smooth.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
