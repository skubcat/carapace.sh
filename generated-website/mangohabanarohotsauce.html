Roasted Habanero Mango Hotsauce

Ingredients

  • 1x Mango
  • 1x Roasted Habanero
  • 5x Small Bellpepers
  • 1x Medium Onion
  • Sugar - 2 Tsps
  • Garlic powder - 1 Tsp
  • Black pepper/Salt - enough to taste

Instructions

  • Roast bellpepers 20 mins at 450F.
  • Vinegar in a pot with ingredients, enough to cover. Boil the mixture for about 5 minutes.
  • Blend until smooth.
